About God's Covering and Son Trucking

Founded in 2008 in Manchester, Jamaica, our family-owned trucking company has grown from a single truck operation to a trusted company contracted throughout the province of Nova Scotia and Canada.

Our mission is to obtain dynamic and challenging positions within any organization, providing reliable, safe, and efficient transportation services while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and customer service. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients based on trust and consistent performance.

"Faith, family, and reliable service are the foundations of our business."

- Kevin Cowie, Founder
